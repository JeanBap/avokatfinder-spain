Spain Golden Visa & Investor Immigration: Routes, Thresholds & Status (2025)

Spain's investor residence programme (Golden Visa) was established by Ley 14/2013 de apoyo a los emprendedores (arts. 63–67). The real estate route (€500,000 minimum) is the most used — but in April 2024 the Sánchez government announced plans to abolish it. As of May 2025, the legislative abolition has not been completed; the route remains technically available.

Golden Visa Investment Thresholds — Ley 14/2013

Investment Type Minimum Permit Political Status (2025)

Ley Beckham / Régimen especial LIRNR art. 93: Golden Visa holders who work in Spain (e.g., as a director of their company) can qualify for the 24% flat rate IRNR for 6 years. Apply via Modelo 149 within 6 months of arriving. Family members (spouse + children under 18) included on same Golden Visa application.

🔍 TL;DR — Spain Golden Visa (2025)
  • Real estate route (€500k): technically still active but government has announced abolition — seek legal advice before purchasing for visa purposes
  • Financial investment routes (€1–2M): stable, no abolition threatened
  • Processing time: 20 business days guaranteed by law (Ley 14/2013)
  • Digital nomad visa (Ley 28/2022): growing alternative for remote workers — no investment needed
  • Ley Beckham régimen especial: 24% flat tax for 6 years — significant tax saving for high earners
