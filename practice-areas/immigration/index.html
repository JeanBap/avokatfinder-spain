Spanish Visas & Residence Permits: Routes, Fees & Timelines (2025)

Spain immigration is governed by the Ley de Extranjería (LO 4/2000) and its Reglamento (RD 557/2011), plus the Ley de Startups (Ley 28/2022) for entrepreneurs and digital nomads. EU nationals exercise free movement — only non-EU nationals require work/residence permits.

Main Residence & Work Permit Routes

Permit Type Law / Art. Duration Key Requirement Official Fee Timeline
Permiso de trabajo + residencia (general) LO 4/2000 art. 36 1 year (renewable) Job offer from Spanish employer; employer applies at SEPE €73.35 1–3 months
Tarjeta azul UE (EU Blue Card) RD 1 557/2011 art. 83 4 years Salary ≥ 1.5× average (≈€46,000/yr 2025); university degree; CDI offer €73.35 2–4 months
Emprendedor / Entrepreneur (Ley Startups) Ley 28/2022 art. 68 3 years Qualifying innovative project; DGI authorisation; no salary minimum €73.35 20 business days (guaranteed)
Nómada digital (Digital Nomad Visa) Ley 28/2022 art. 74 1 year (→ 3 year TIE) Remote work for non-Spanish employer(s); income ≥ 200% SMI (≈€2,268/mo) €73 (visa) + €16 (TIE) 20 business days
Visado no lucrativo (Non-lucrative) RD 557/2011 art. 46 1 year (renewable) Passive income ≥ €28,800/yr (2×IPREM×14); no work in Spain €73 3–6 months
Golden Visa — Real estate Ley 14/2013 art. 63 2 years → 5 years Property purchase ≥ €500,000 free of mortgage/charges €500–3,000 (abogado) 20 business days
Arraigo social / laboral (Regularisation) RD 557/2011 art. 124 1 year 3 years illegal residence (social) or 2 years + work contract (laboral) €73.35 3 months

Under Ley Beckham / Startup Act régimen especial: pays IRNR 24% on Spanish income (not progressive IRPF up to 47%) for 5 years. Must apply within 6 months of arriving via Modelo 149. Income threshold: 200% SMI = €2,268/month minimum (based on 2024 SMI €1,134).

🔍 TL;DR — Spanish Immigration for Expats
  • Digital nomad visa (Ley 28/2022): fastest route for remote workers — 20 business days, income ≥ 200% SMI
  • Startup Act entrepreneur: DGI authorisation in 20 business days (guaranteed by law)
  • Golden Visa (real estate): €500k+ property, 2-year permit — political uncertainty about abolition as of 2025
  • Ley Beckham / régimen especial: 24% flat IRNR instead of IRPF for 5 years — apply via Modelo 149 within 6 months
  • NIE is mandatory for all property purchases, bank accounts, and company registration in Spain
