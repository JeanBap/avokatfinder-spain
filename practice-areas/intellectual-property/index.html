Spanish Intellectual Property: OEPM Registration Costs & Enforcement (2025)

Spanish IP is governed by the Ley de Marcas (LM 17/2001), Ley de Patentes (LP 24/2015), and the Real Decreto Legislativo 1/1996 (LPI — Copyright). Registration is administered by the OEPM (Oficina Española de Patentes y Marcas). Copyright arises automatically — no registration required (LPI art. 1).

Juzgados de lo Mercantil: IP cases in Spain are tried by the Juzgados Mercantiles — Barcelona and Madrid have specialist IP sections. Barcelona is particularly known for software/copyright disputes; Madrid for trademark. Since UPC launch in June 2023, European patents can be litigated centrally.

🔍 TL;DR — Spanish IP for International Businesses
  • Trademark: OEPM €146.75 (1 class) — file EU mark (EUIPO €850) for broader coverage
  • Modelo de utilidad: Spain's utility model (10 years) — faster and cheaper than a full patent
  • Copyright: automatic, no registration needed — for proof of date, use OEPM record or notarial act
  • Check OEPM database (oepm.es) before filing any trademark — free search
  • UPC since 2023: European patents in Spain can now be litigated in the unified court system
